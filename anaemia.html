Anaemia



Anaemia is the deficiency disease of Iron. If we don't eat enough amount of Iron, it leads to this condition called Anaemia.


The symptoms of anaemia are:-

1. Low energy levels, 2. lose of appetite, 3. paleness, 4. reduces growth.



We need to eat iron-rich as the following below


Green leafy vegetables, apple, jaggery,figs and guava.
