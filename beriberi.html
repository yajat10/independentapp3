Beriberi



Beriberi is the deficiency disease of Vitamin B1. If we don't eat enough amount of Vitamin B1, it leads to this condition called Beriberi.


The symptoms of Beriberi are:-

1. weakening of muscles,
2. degeneration of nerves.
