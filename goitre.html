Goitre is the deficiency disease of Iodine. If we don't eat enough amount of Iodine, it leads to this condition called Goitre.


The symptoms of Goitre are:-


1. Swelling of the thiroid gland(Present in the neck), 2. poor growth in infacy stage and metal retardation.



We need to eat iodine-rich food as the following below


1. Sea food, 2. Iodised salt.
