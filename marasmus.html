Marasmus



Marasmus is the deficiency disease of protein and carbohydrate. If we don't eat enough amount of proteins and carbohydrate, it leads to this condition called Marasmus.

The symptoms of marasmus are:-

1. thinning
2. weight lose
3. loose folds of skin
