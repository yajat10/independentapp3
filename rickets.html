Rickets



Rickets are the deficiency disease of Vitamin D. If we don't eat enough amount of Vitamin D, it leads to this condition called Rickets.


The symptoms of Rickets are:-

1. Softening and weakening of bones,
2. bow-shaped legs and Easily breakable bones.
