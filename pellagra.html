Pellagra



Pellagra is the deficiency disease of Vitamin B2. If we don't eat enough amount of Vitamin B2, it leads to this condition called Pellagra.


The symptoms of Pellagra are:-

1. Inflammation of skin,
2. Diarrhoea,
3. Memory disorder.
