Diarrhoea



Diarrhoea is frequent, loose, and watery bowel movements. Bowel movements, also called stools, are body wastes passed through the rectum and anus. Stools contain what is left after your digestive system absorbs nutrients and fluids from what you eat and drink.


Images of infected patients


We need to eat the following food during Diarrhoea

1. bananas

2. hot cereals, such as oatmeal, cream of wheat, or rice porridge.

3. boiled potatoes and bread or toast.
