Scurvy



Scurvy is a deficiency disease of Vitamin C . If we don't eat enough amount of Vitamin C, it leads to this condition called Scurvy.


The symptoms of scurvy are:-

1. Bleeding gums,
2. internal bleeding and delay wound healing.
